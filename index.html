About The Author

Matthew Layton

Founder & Software Architect

Matthew is a seasoned software developer with several years of professional experience whose education and career have consistently focused on delivering highly functional, quality, pragmatic and elegant enterprise software solutions. Formerly the Technical Lead for Blockchain and DLT at TradeIX and now Technical and Innovation Lead at Ivno, his leadership and software architecture experience includes several years working with Distributed Ledger Technology, specifically in the Corda ecosystem. Matthew led the creation and delivery of significant milestones for TradeIX's Marco Polo Platform and Ivno's Token Collateralization Platform.

From a young age, Matthew has been passionate about programming and understanding programming languages with meticulous and scrupulous detail. As a Corda certified professional developer and an IBM certified Blockchain developer, Matthew has a keen interest in applied cryptography, especially working in the Blockchain and DLT ecosystem.

Matthew is in the top 1% of StackOverflow's 100 million users for answering and educating participants, reaching over 4.5 million people.
