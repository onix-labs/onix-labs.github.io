Corda Identity Framework

Comprehensive digital identity management for Corda

Influenced by the W3C specifications for decentralized identities and verifiable credentials, this framework provides a powerful and comprehensive API surface for building highly scalable, interoperable, digital and decentralized identities for individuals, organisations and assets on Corda. Fundamentally this framework represents a protocol for interoperable data exchange between different Corda applications.

View on GitHub

Corda Business Network Management System

A platform for creating and managing business networks on Corda

The BNMS is designed to provide membership and multi-lateral relationship management services for distributed, interoperable business networks on Corda. By building on top of the Corda Identity Framework, the BNMS is flexible by design and requires minimal developer implementation into existing Corda applications, allowing fully customizable membership and relationship models which can be tailored to meet specific business and network requirements.
